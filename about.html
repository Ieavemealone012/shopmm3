About MM3

MM3 Merch is the official store connected to the MM3 Roblox game and its development studio. Everything here is created for fans, players, and supporters who want to own something tied directly to the project.

What is MM3?

MM3 was created and founded by Leaf — known on Roblox as leavemealone012 — back in March 2021. The game, brand, and merch store are all part of the same project built from the ground up by the developer! The merch stores exists as an extension of that world, A place where players can support the game and get something cool in return!

Why This Store Exists

Instead of using random third-party sellers or low-quality providers, this store was made to give players a safe, official place to buy items related to the MM3 universe. Everything here is created, approved, and released directly by the studio.

Created by the Developer

Every product in the store is personally handled by the developer behind MM3 - from design, to quality checks, to distribution. No outside companies, no generic templates, no mass-produced junk. Just authentic stuff connected to the game and its community!

Have Questions?

If you need help with an order, have feedback, or want to ask something about the shop, head over to the contact page. Support for the game itself should still go through the usual Discord channels or in-game feedback system! — this form is only for store-related stuff.
