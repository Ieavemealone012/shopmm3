MM3 Shipping & Refund Policy

Shipping Information

All orders are processed within 3-5 business days. Shipping times may vary depending on your location. We ship to most countries, but international shipping may take longer.

You will receive a confirmation email once your order has been shipped, including tracking information if available.

Refunds & Returns

If you are not satisfied with your purchase, you may request a refund or exchange within 14 days of receiving your order. Please ensure the item is unused and in its original packaging.

To request a return, contact us via the Contact page with your order details. Refunds will be processed within 5-7 business days after we receive the returned item.

Exceptions

Certain items such as limited editions or custom merchandise may not be eligible for a return. Refund eligibility will be confirmed when contacting support.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about shipping, returns, or refunds, please use our Contact page to get in touch.
